# Art attribution

## Hero & skeleton (LPC)
`hero/*` (body, hair, leather/chain armor, sword/heavy weapon) and `skeleton/*`
are composited from the **Liberated Pixel Cup (LPC)** Universal Spritesheet
collection — Sander Frenken's "Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator"
(github.com/sanderfrenken/Universal-LPC-Spritesheet-Character-Generator).

LPC assets are licensed **CC-BY-SA 4.0 / GPL 3.0**, contributed by many artists
(Stephen Challener "Redshrike", Johannes Sjölund "wulax", Matthew Krohn, and
others — see each layer's credits in the LPC repo). Derivatives (these cropped
single-frame layers) remain under the same CC-BY-SA/GPL terms.

## Goblin
`goblin2/*` — CC0 animated goblin by *thekingphoenix* (OpenGameArt).

## Backgrounds
Painterly backgrounds are public-domain artworks (John Bauer, Vilhelm Hammershøi)
and imported project art.
